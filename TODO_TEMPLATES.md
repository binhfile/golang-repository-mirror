## How to Use These Templates

1. **Copy the template** that matches your task type
2. **Customize the task names** to match your specific situation
3. **Add or remove tasks** as needed for your project
4. **Update content and activeForm** to be specific to your work
5. **Create the todo list** using `TodoWrite` at the start of work
6. **Update status** as you work through each task
